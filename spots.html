Favourite spots

Kashmir

Kashmir is considered a favorite place to visit for a variety of reasons, including its natural beauty, rich cultural heritage, and adventure activities. Firstly, Kashmir is famous for its stunning natural beauty, which includes snow-capped mountains, sparkling lakes, lush green valleys, and picturesque landscapes. The region is also known for its diverse flora and fauna, including rare species like the Kashmir stag, snow leopard, and black bear.

Hunza

Hunza is a popular tourist destination in the northern region of Pakistan, known for its stunning natural beauty, rich culture and history, and friendly people.

Murree

Murree is situated in the foothills of the Himalayas, which offers spectacular scenic beauty to visitors. The lush green forests, scenic valleys, and the cool climate make it an ideal destination for tourists.

Switzerland

Switzerland is known for its stunning natural beauty, including the Swiss Alps, lakes, and picturesque towns and villages. It is also famous for its high quality of life, efficient public transportation, and strong economy. These factors, along with a rich cultural heritage and diverse population, make Switzerland a desirable place to visit or live for many people.

Noor Mahal

Noor Mahal is known for its stunning architecture, which blends traditional Indian and modern styles. The hotel features grand courtyards, beautiful gardens, and spacious rooms and suites, making it a luxurious and comfortable place to stay. The hotel's interior is decorated with intricate carvings, hand-painted murals, and other ornate details that create a sense of grandeur and opulence..

Badshahi Mosque

Badshahi Mosque is a historical and cultural landmark located in the city of Lahore, in the Punjab province of Pakistan. It was built in 1673 by the Mughal Emperor Aurangzeb, and is one of the largest mosques in the world. The mosque is an impressive example of Mughal architecture, with intricate designs and details that are a testament to the skill and craftsmanship of the builders and artisans of the time.
